# StreetRise Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** [[EFFECTIVE_DATE]]

This Privacy Policy explains how **[[LEGAL_ENTITY]]** ("StreetRise," "we," "us,"
or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the StreetRise
mobile app and the website at app.streetrise.org (together, the "Service").

StreetRise helps people find verified local support services — such as shelter,
food, hygiene, and related resources — and helps service providers keep that
information accurate. We are committed to protecting the privacy and dignity of
everyone who uses the Service, especially people in vulnerable situations.

> **Plain-language summary:** You can browse resources and use the map without an
> account. We ask for your location only to show help near you. We send
> notifications only if you allow them. We do not sell your personal information
> and we do not track you across other apps or websites. We do not collect your
> payment card details — donations are handled by Stripe.

---

## 1. Information we collect

### a. Location information
With your permission, we access your device's **approximate and/or precise
location** to show nearby resources, center the map, and provide directions. You
can use much of the Service without granting location access, and you can revoke
the permission at any time in your device settings. We do not continuously track
your location in the background.

### b. Account information (only if you create an account)
Some features — primarily for service providers and administrators — require an
account. When you register or sign in, we collect your **email address** and any
profile details you provide (such as your name or organization). Authentication
is handled through our backend provider, Supabase.

### c. Activity you submit
If you are signed in, we collect information you actively provide, such as
**booking or service requests**, listing updates (for providers), and similar
interactions, so the Service can function.

### d. Notification token
If you allow notifications, we register a **push notification token** (via Google
Firebase Cloud Messaging) so we can deliver alerts you opted into, such as bed
availability and service updates. This token identifies your device for
messaging only.

### e. Information collected automatically
To operate and secure the Service, our servers may process standard technical
data such as IP address and basic device/connection information. We do **not** use
third-party advertising or cross-app tracking technologies.

### f. Information we do NOT collect
We do **not** collect your payment card or bank account details. Donations and
payments, if any, are processed by **Stripe** in your device's web browser;
Stripe's handling of that information is governed by Stripe's privacy policy.

## 2. How we use information
We use the information described above to:

- Show you relevant resources near your location and provide directions;
- Provide and maintain the map, search, and filtering features;
- Create and manage accounts and authenticate provider/admin users;
- Process and coordinate booking or service requests;
- Send notifications you have opted into;
- Maintain the security, integrity, and reliability of the Service;
- Comply with legal obligations.

We do **not** sell your personal information, and we do **not** use your data for
advertising or to track you across other companies' apps or websites.

---

## 3. How information is shared
We share information only as needed to run the Service:

- **Service providers (processors):** vendors that host and power the Service,
  including Supabase (backend, authentication, and database) and Google Firebase
  Cloud Messaging (push notifications). They process data on our behalf.
- **Service listings:** the resource directory contains information about
  third-party organizations (e.g., name, address, phone, hours). This is publicly
  available service information, not your personal data.
- **Legal reasons:** if required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect
  the rights, safety, and security of users or the public.
- **Business transfers:** in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of
  assets, subject to this Policy.

We do not share your personal information with other users of the app.

---

## 4. Data retention
We keep personal information only as long as needed to provide the Service and for
legitimate legal and operational purposes. Browsing the resource directory without
an account does not create a personal profile. Account data is retained until you
request deletion or your account is closed.
