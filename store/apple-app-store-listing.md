## Description
> Max 4000 characters

```
StreetRise helps people experiencing homelessness — and the outreach workers,
case managers, and volunteers who support them — find verified local support
services in seconds.

Open the app to see a live, map-based directory of nearby resources: emergency
shelters, free meals, hygiene and shower facilities, day centers, medical care,
and more. Every listing includes the essentials you need on the street: address,
phone number, hours, and one-tap directions.

WHAT YOU CAN DO WITH STREETRISE

• Discover nearby help — An interactive map centers on your location and shows
  the closest shelter, food, hygiene, and support services.

• Filter by need — Instantly narrow results by category, such as emergency
  shelter, day-use, free meals, or hygiene services.

• Act in one tap — Call a provider directly, or launch turn-by-turn directions
  in your preferred maps app.

• Stay informed — Opt in to notifications about bed availability, openings, and
  important service updates.

• Works when you need it — Core resource information is available even on slow or
  spotty connections.

FOR SERVICE PROVIDERS

StreetRise is also a coordination platform for shelters and community
organizations. Verified providers can manage their listings, update real-time
bed and resource availability, and respond to booking requests — so the
information people rely on stays accurate.

BUILT FOR DIGNITY AND TRUST

• Verified information — Listings are reviewed to keep details accurate.
• Privacy-first — We ask for location only to show nearby help, and you stay in
  control of permissions.
• No cost to find help — StreetRise is free for people seeking services.

StreetRise currently focuses on Central Florida and is expanding.

Questions or feedback? Contact us at [[CONTACT_EMAIL]].
```

## Age Rating questionnaire (expected answers)
Answer **None / No** to all of: cartoon/realistic violence, sexual content,
profanity, horror, gambling, alcohol/drug references, mature themes. Unrestricted
web access: the app loads `app.streetrise.org` and may open external links in the
system browser → answer **Yes** to "Unrestricted Web Access" if asked. Expected
rating: **4+** (or **17+** if you flag unrestricted web access; choose based on
review feedback).

## ⚠️ App Store review risk — Guideline 4.2 (Minimum Functionality)

Apple frequently rejects apps that are primarily a wrapper around a website. To
clear review, the listing and the build should emphasize **native** value:
native map + device location, push notifications, one-tap calling/directions via
native intents, and offline resource access. Make sure these are working and
visible in your screenshots and reviewer notes.
